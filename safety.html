ECU ALERT

ECU Alert is ECU’s collection of communication tools used to distribute emergency notification and information to campus. It is only used for emergencies and occasional required testing. Individuals can register to receive critical information via text message and email, and can download the ECU LiveSafe app from the page linked below.
