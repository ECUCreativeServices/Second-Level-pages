Dr. Ron Mitchelson

Interim Vice Chancellor for Health Sciences

Dr. Ron Mitchelson was named interim vice chancellor of ECU’s Division of Health Sciences in May 2021. He previously served ECU as provost, senior vice chancellor of academic affairs and, most recently, as interim chancellor of the university from October 2019 until March 2021.

Mitchelson arrived at ECU in 1999 as a professor and chair of the Department of Geography. Before coming to Greenville, he held faculty and administrative positions at the University of Georgia and at Morehead State University in Kentucky.

While serving as ECU’s provost from 2015 until accepting the interim chancellor position in 2019, Mitchelson served as the university’s chief academic officer with oversight of academic programming, enrollment management, institutional planning and research, and equity and diversity.

He earned doctoral degrees in geography and economic geography from The Ohio State University in 1979. He received a bachelor’s degree in geography and economics from The State University of New York (SUNY) at Buffalo in 1975.
